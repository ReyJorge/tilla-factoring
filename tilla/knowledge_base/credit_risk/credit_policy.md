# TILLA Credit Policy (DRAFT — NOT APPROVED FOR PRODUCTION)

**Status:** Internal MVP placeholder. **Do not use for binding decisions** until reviewed by TILLA Risk Committee and Legal.

This draft describes high-level principles for **supplier invoice financing** with **anchor (debtor) acceptance**. Nothing here constitutes legal advice.

## Principles

1. **Know Your Counterparty:** Verified ICO/DIČ, beneficial ownership screening (outside this MVP scope — manual).
2. **Anchor strength dominates:** Weak anchor profile implies tighter supplier limits regardless of supplier standing.
3. **Concentration:** Single-anchor or single-supplier concentration triggers escalation (human review).
4. **Documentation:** Credit memo required before funding release for material exposures.

## Escalation

Any scoring grade **D**, material overdue narrative, or advance above policy corridor requires **human review**.
